Introduction

This guide explains the complete method, setup, and hidden features for the ATMA web application. Please review this document carefully to ensure a smooth and effective performance.

Method

ATMA is a unique application designed to create a powerful and modern mentalism effect. The method is designed for situations where you use a pre-printed QR code (e.g., on a business card or a mysterious photograph).

As you present the choices to the spectator, the app sends those selections to an online database in *real-time* in the background. The QR code you have printed links to a special webpage that reads the data from the database to display a news article corresponding to the spectator's choices. This method requires an active internet connection on your phone during the performance.

Settings Menu

To access the settings menu, simply tap the top-right corner of the screen at any time. The first time you open the app, you will be required to set up your Custom ID. Inside, you will find the following options:

  • Custom ID (Mandatory):
    • Set a unique, secret 6-digit number. This ID is crucial as it links your app to the database, creating your personal "channel" that the printed QR code will listen to.
    • Change ID: You can change your ID at any time. Warning: Changing your ID will render your old QR code non-functional. You must download and print the new QR code image to match the new ID.
    • Download QR: Once an ID is saved, this button will open a new tab with the QR Code Generator page. On this page, you will see a preview of a newspaper article that contains your unique QR code. You can select the language for the article text and then use the download button on that page to save the complete article as a high-quality PNG image, ready to be printed.
    • Delete ID: This button permanently removes the currently entered ID and its data from the database. Use with caution.
  • Language Selection: Choose between English and Indonesian. This changes the language of the revelation article that the spectator sees.

Input Method

ATMA uses a clever gesture-based system to register choices. The method is designed to look natural and unsuspicious, as you perform it openly. The key lies in the STARTING AREA of a single SWIPE LEFT gesture.

The screen is divided into five invisible zones. Where you begin your swipe determines which choice is registered. Since the spectator sees the app as a fullscreen photo gallery, swiping left is the most natural action to see the "next photo".

Performance Flow

  1. Pre-Show Setup: Before performing, open the app's Settings. Set your secret 6-digit Custom ID. Click the Download QR button, which will take you to the generator page. On this new page, select your desired language for the article's appearance and download the final image. Print this image (containing the QR code) on a business card, photograph, or other object.
  2. Introduce the Premise: Introduce the experiment. Present the lists of choices (Victim, Location, Weapon) to the spectator. Keep the object with the printed QR code hidden for now.
  3. Input Choices: As the spectator makes their choices, show them the corresponding list on your phone. To move to the next list, simply perform a SWIPE LEFT gesture from the area corresponding to their choice. The action will look completely natural, as if you are just moving to the next photo in a gallery.
  4. Climax: After the third and final swipe, the app on your phone automatically updates the database. Now, reveal the pre-printed object (the business card, photo, etc.) and ask the spectator to scan the QR code. Their phone will open the news article, which will contain their freely chosen details. The final screen on your phone will show an image, perfect for misdirection.

System Controls & Features

  • Open Settings: Tap the top-right corner of the screen.
  • Reset App: To reset for a new performance, perform a quick triple-tap on the final screen (the image). This action prepares the app for a new set of inputs.
  • View Input History: To confirm your inputs, press and hold your finger on the final screen. An overlay showing your registered choices will appear and will vanish when you lift your finger.

Security & Plausibility

This feature is designed to protect the method if a spectator becomes suspicious and re-scans the QR code after the performance.

At the start of a performance, the app automatically sets your channel's status to "Performing". After the climax, perform a quick double-tap on the final image screen. This sets your channel's status to "Not Performing" in the background. If the spectator re-scans the printed QR code later, the article will appear in a locked, static state, protecting the secret of the real-time update.

Operational Tips

  • Lock Screen Rotation: Before performing, ensure your phone's screen rotation is locked to prevent layout shifts.
  • Practice: Rehearse the gesture sequences until they become automatic. Your actions should appear as if you are simply sliding through photos or pages.
